# English Corrections — 2026-03-17

---

#### "지금 다시 돌렸어 지금 정석적으로 개발하고 있는건가"
- Better English: "I restarted it. Are we developing this the proper way?"
- Why: "the proper way" captures "정석적으로" better than "orthodox"

#### "로그는 다 sql이랑 api랑 다 따로 놀도록해"
- Better English: "Separate the SQL logs from the API logs — they shouldn't be mixed."
- Why: "따로 놀다" = "be separated" in technical English

#### "로그가 계속 보이게 어찌하냐 터미널에서"
- Better English: "How do I keep the logs streaming in the terminal?"
- Why: "streaming" is the standard term for continuous log output
